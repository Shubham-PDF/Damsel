Hellew Damsel
A Cute Little Damsel
Teri manmohak si khushbu, hawaon mein ghul jaati hai,
Meri saanson se lipat kar, mujhe bas teri yaad dilati hai.
Tu paas ho ya na ho, magar ehsaas tera saath rehta hai,
Har dhadkan mein tera naghma, har pal tujhse baatein kehta hai.

Tera chehra, noor ka ek ujala hai,
Jo sitaron se bhi zyada chamakta nirala hai.
Kya ye chand bhi tujhi se roshni leta hai ?
tera noor jo chand ko bhi piche chodh deta hai !

Tere saath mera dil ek madhosh geet gungunata hai,
Tere saath pura din bhi pal mai beet jata hai.
Suryodaya ki koyal si teri pyari pukar hai,
Tu kuch na bhi bole, to bhi mere sir par sawaar hai.

Will you be my valentine?
